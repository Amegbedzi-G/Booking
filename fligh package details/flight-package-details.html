Reschedule Information

How to Reschedule Your Flight:

  • Access Your Booking: Log in to your account or use your booking reference and last name to access your flight details.
  • Select New Dates: Choose your preferred new travel date(s) and time(s). Flight availability and fare differences may apply.
  • Review Fare Differences: If your new flight has a higher fare, the difference will be added to the total cost. If it's lower, the balance will be refunded or credited.
  • Confirm Changes: Once you're satisfied with your new selection, confirm the changes. You will receive a confirmation email with your updated flight details.

Important Notes:

  • Rescheduling is subject to availability and airline policies.
  • Fees may apply, depending on the airline and fare class.
  • We’re committed to ensuring your travel experience remains flexible and hassle-free.

Important Considerations:

  • Availability: All reschedules are subject to seat availability on the new flight dates. We recommend booking changes as early as possible to secure your preferred schedule.
  • Booking Restrictions: Some promotional or economy-class tickets may have restrictions on changes. Please refer to your booking conditions or contact customer support for clarification.
